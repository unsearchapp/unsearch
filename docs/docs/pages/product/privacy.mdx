# Privacy Policy

The Unsearch browser extension ("Unsearch") provides functionality to synchronize your bookmarks, search history, and tabs across devices.
This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data.

## Information We Collect

1. Account Information:
   We collect your email address, password, and information related to your user plan when you create an account. This data is stored securely in our database.

2. Bookmarks, Search History, and Tabs:
   The Unsearch browser extension collects your bookmarks, search history, and open tabs to provide synchronization functionality. This data is sent from the extension to our server and stored in our database.
   All your sensitive data is stored encrypted.

## How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Unsearch functionality and services. This includes synchronizing your data across devices and managing your account.

## Data Security

Sensitive data is encrypted and stored in our database located in the EU, ensuring compliance with strict EU privacy regulations.

## Information Sharing

We will never share or sell your personal information to third parties. Your data will only be used for the purposes described in this policy.

## Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data. You can exercise these rights by contacting us.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at contact@unsearch.app.
